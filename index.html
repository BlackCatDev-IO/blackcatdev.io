About

software developer

sound engineer

black cat dad

As an NYC based live sound engineer working all over the world for the last 18 years, when the entire event production industry shut down in 2020, I found myself with nothing but time to learn some new skills. I was fortunate to find two new passions in life. Playing piano, and software development.

While I'd be happy to do either one as a full time career, I think we all can agree the latter is the safer choice. So while concerts and corporate events have thankfully returned, software is now my main focus.

Both my previous industry and programming are very techincally demanding fields which require that one always stay on top of rapidly emerging technologies and industry standards, or get left behind.

Never become complacent, never stop learning.

My name is Loren Aguey and l look forward to working with you.
